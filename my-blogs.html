My sajek tour

Author:GM Shimon

The trip will start from khagrachari Twon. 7 AM: Breakfast will be served at a local restaurant at Khagrachori town. After breakfast our guide will pick you up with a 4x4 Land Cruiser or Chander Gari. 8 AM: Journey to Sajek Valley by Chander Gari. It’ll take 2 hours to reach Bhagaichori from where Army will escort the vehicle to Sajek Valley. From Bhagaichori, it’ll take another 2 hours to reach the final destination, Sajek Valley.12 PM: Reached at Sajek Valley and check into the eco resort. 2 PM: Lunch will be served at a local restaurant with standard Bengali menu plain rice, vhorta, vegetable, chicken curry, dal, water at Sajek Valley. 3 PM: Visiting Konglok Para. It is highest point of Sajek valley. There’s a small village on the top of Konglok Para. So, you’ll experience the tribal lifestyle. 6 PM: Enjoy mesmerizing sunset from the Helipad of Sajek. The view from the helipad is something unique & amazing. 9 PM: Dinner will be served with Chicken BBQ, Paratha & Soft drinks. Night will be spent at the resort in a shared room.

Rangamati

Author:GM Shimon

Rangamati is a small town of the Chittagong Division and a district in south-eastern Bangladesh. Rangamati is covered with green hills, lakes, and rivers. For this reason, the tourist from Bangladesh and abroad like the place for passing the free time. In general, it is situated at the side of Kaptai Lake, that’s why Rangamati is considered as the Lake City Of Bangladesh. Important to realize, it is the largest district in Bangladesh is just 77 km away from Chittagong town. For the tradition of the tribal people, scenic beauty, type of locations and natural resources including diverse flora and fauna have made the place more attractive. The tour of Travel Mate will give you a special opportunity to enjoy the Rangamati tour more closely. In addition, you have the most chances to meet with the tribal villages including Chakma, Marma, Tonchongya, Tripura, Murong, Bome, Khumi, Kheyang, Chak, Pankhoa, and Lusai. Culture and diverse traditions will give you an exciting experience. The beauty of the hilly areas, lake and greeny location, will give the amazing feeling to go very near to nature.
